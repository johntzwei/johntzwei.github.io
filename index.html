Hi! I am a graduate student interested in language + computation.

About

I am second year PhD student focusing on natural language processing and machine learning, studying at the University of Southern California, where I am advised by Nanyun Peng. My current interest is in the robustness of automatic evaluation metrics. Previously, I earned a B.S. in Mathematics at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. When there are slow weeks at school, I can be found lifting myself at the USC Parkside / Vineyard recreation center calisthenic parks or riding my bikes to places within driving distance. I also try to read different books, as much as I tried to include reading in this paragraph.
